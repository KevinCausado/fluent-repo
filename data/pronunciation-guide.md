# Pronunciation Guide — Anchor Words
*(English sounds that don't exist in Spanish)*

| Sound | Anchor Word | Spanish Approximation | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| TH sorda | **THINK** | como *z* suave, lengua entre dientes sin voz | think, three, thank, month, both |
| TH sonora | **THE** | como *d* suave, lengua entre dientes con voz | the, they, that, this, those |
| Schwa | **ABOUT** | vocal neutra *uh*, sin estrés, sin color | about, problem, family, camera, today |
| Vocal æ | **CAT** | entre *a* y *e*, más abierta que ambas | cat, bad, man, hat, black |
| Vocal corta ʌ | **CUT** | *a* corta y central, no la *a* española | cut, but, love, come, enough |
| R americana | **BIRD** | *r* retroflexa, lengua doblada hacia atrás, no vibra | bird, word, her, turn, learn |
